2000s - Social Media

Platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and YouTube changed online interactions.

Social media has become a crucial part of modern communication.

Evolution of Social Media

Social media started with simple platforms like MySpace and Friendster. It evolved into complex networks like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and TikTok.

Impact on Society

  • Communication: Social media has transformed how we communicate, allowing instant sharing of thoughts, photos, and videos.
  • Business: Companies use social media for marketing, customer service, and brand building.
  • Politics: Social media plays a significant role in political campaigns and movements.

Challenges

While social media has many benefits, it also poses challenges like privacy concerns, misinformation, and mental health issues.
