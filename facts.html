Did You Know?

There are over 1.5 billion websites on the internet, and the first email was sent in 1971!

Internet Usage

As of 2021, there are over 4.9 billion internet users worldwide, which is more than half of the global population.

First Website

The first website, created by Tim Berners-Lee, is still online. It was launched on August 6, 1991, and provided information about the World Wide Web project.

Internet Speed

The fastest internet speed ever recorded is 319 terabits per second (Tbps), achieved by researchers in Japan in 2021.

Domain Names

The first domain name ever registered was symbolics.com on March 15, 1985. It is still active today.

Internet of Things (IoT)

By 2025, it is estimated that there will be over 75 billion IoT devices connected to the internet, ranging from smart home devices to industrial sensors.

Search Engines

Google processes over 3.5 billion searches per day, making it the most popular search engine in the world.

Social Media

Facebook, the largest social media platform, has over 2.8 billion monthly active users as of 2021.
